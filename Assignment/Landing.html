Tuberculosis is now the world’s leading cause of death from an infectious disease. “HeroRATs” help.

APOPO trains African giant pouched rats to rapidly detect tuberculosis in human sputum samples. These highly accurate, non-invasive screenings help identify cases often missed by conventional tests. By combining innovative science with an unlikely partner, APOPO saves lives and speeds up access to treatment.
